Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GhibliAI

General Questions

What is GhibliAI?

GhibliAI is an advanced AI tool that transforms your photos into artwork inspired by Studio Ghibli's distinctive animation style. Using state-of-the-art generative adversarial networks (GANs), our system analyzes your images and recreates them with the magical, hand-painted look that Ghibli films are famous for.

Is GhibliAI affiliated with Studio Ghibli?

No, GhibliAI is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Studio Ghibli in any way. We're simply fans creating a tool that pays homage to their distinctive art style. All original Studio Ghibli artwork and intellectual property belongs to Studio Ghibli.

How accurate are the transformations?

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of frames from Studio Ghibli films to capture their distinctive style. While no AI can perfectly replicate the magic of hand-drawn animation by master artists, our system creates impressive transformations that capture the essence, color palette, and visual characteristics of Ghibli's artwork.

Technical Questions

What image formats are supported?

GhibliAI supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP image formats. For optimal results, we recommend using high-quality images with clear subjects and good lighting.

Is there a size limit for uploaded images?

Yes, the maximum file size for uploads is 10MB. Images must be at least 1000px in width for the best results. If your image is larger, you may need to resize it before uploading.

How does the AI technology work?

GhibliAI uses a custom implementation of StyleGAN3 neural network architecture, specifically optimized for Ghibli-style transformations. Our model was trained on over 50,000 carefully selected frames from Studio Ghibli films, learning to recognize and recreate the distinctive features, color palettes, and brush stroke patterns that define the Ghibli look.

The transformation process happens in multiple stages:

  1. Image analysis and composition detection
  2. Subject recognition and separation
  3. Stylistic transformation of subjects and backgrounds
  4. Color palette adjustment
  5. Texture and brush stroke application
  6. Final enhancement and detailing

Pricing Questions

Can I try GhibliAI for free?

Yes! Our free plan allows you to transform up to 5 images per month at standard resolution. This is a great way to test our service and see the quality of transformations before committing to a paid plan.

What do the premium plans include?

Our premium plans offer additional features and higher usage limits:

  • Premium ($9.99/month): 100 transformations per month, high resolution output, all Ghibli styles, no watermarks, and basic setting controls.
  • Professional ($19.99/month): Unlimited transformations, ultra HD resolution, exclusive styles, no watermarks, full setting controls, and priority processing.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing cycle, at which point your account will revert to the free plan.

Usage Questions

How long does the transformation process take?

Most transformations are completed within 5-10 seconds, depending on the complexity of the image and current server load. Professional plan users receive priority processing for even faster results.

What kind of images work best with GhibliAI?

GhibliAI works best with clear, well-lit images with distinct subjects. Landscapes, portraits, and nature scenes tend to produce the most impressive results. The system can handle complex images, but simpler compositions often yield more dramatic transformations.

Can I use the transformed images commercially?

The images you create with GhibliAI are for personal use only. Commercial use is not permitted as the style is inspired by Studio Ghibli's artwork, which is protected by copyright. Additionally, you must own the rights to the original images you upload for transformation.

Are my uploaded images kept private?

Yes, privacy is important to us. Your uploaded images and the resulting transformations are private and only accessible to you. We do not use your images for training our AI or share them with third parties without your consent.
